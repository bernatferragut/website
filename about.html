About Bernat

Media artist pioneering digital arts during it's early VFX years now focusing on the origins of programming and creation through the generative arts, the intersection of philosophy, arts and science.

About

My work as a digital artist starts almost 15 years ago.

From the beginning my interest was purely focused on digital art so I became a professional VFX artist. I programmed art with 3D-Sofware for several independent experimental short-films and worked for big Hollywood Companies producing several AAA movies.

Years later, the Internet changed a lot and I moved to this new fascinating medium: the web. I worked largely as an independent freelance designer and my art slowly became oriented towards purely programmable art.

My interest in computer science and generative art pushed me to go much further into my studies and explore History, Philosophy and Anthropology to reach the seminal LLULL (S.XII-Medieval Europe).

From the last 5 years my work in this direction has been increasing and now I am purely producing art related with the origins of computer science and it’s consequences to today’s digital arts through generative art.

My evolution as a digital artist is entering a period of discovery inside other ‘potential realities’. The next Exhibition I am preparing is focusing on past ‘Out Of the Body Experiences’ and how digitally I can express these out of the world realities and it’s messages to our today’s society.

The Experimentation with new Technologies based on the web 3.0 or Blockchain will be the foundation of these future exhibitions on the web.
