2016 ART EXHIBITION

Art Context:

My explorations on the origins of digital arts through computer sciences brought me to the genius of LLULL.

In partnership with the Cercle Culturel Catalan du Quebec I produced a set of 20 works showing the essential ideas contained in his seminal works about Computational Thinking.

The results were presented dans la Bibiotheque d’arts et Sciences de Montreal for 4 months during September 2016 until early January 2017 with great success.
