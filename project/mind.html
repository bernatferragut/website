2016 ART EXHIBITION

MIND is probably the most violent and visceral of my exhibitions. After travelling for a year through Latin-America and exploring deeply the land of Mexico, I got involved in some deep and intense experiences which showed me a layer of the reality which exists, is real and is made to control our minds and the whole of our society: FEAR.

The Exhibition went trough a deep study of my Christian roots, my Buddhist inpirational practices like meditation and how the mind creates our reality uncounscioussly unless we start to be aware of our own thoughts.

The whole exhibition could be resumed with the first verse of the buddhist text the Dhammapada:

THE MIND

DEEP INTO THE MIND

Mind precedes all knowables, mind’s their chief, mind-made are they. If with a corrupted mind one should either speak or act dukkha follows caused by that, as does the wheel the ox’s hoof. —

MIND ON BEHANCE
