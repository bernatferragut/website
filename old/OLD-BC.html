![](/images/art/BC/BC-intro.png) ## 2016-17 BLOCKCHAIN DISCOVERY Once I discovered the usage and importance of the [Blockchain](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blockchain) Technologyeverything changed to me. Because grasping the general concepts it takes more time than more obvious ones like the Internet Of Things - [IOT](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Internet_of_things) or Artifical Intelligence - [AI](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Artificial_intelligence), once is grasped you cannot stop thinking in how disruptive and game changing will be in the upcoming years. This excitement inspired me to create a first batch of images trying to capture the main underlying concepts of the Blockchain: * DECENTRALISATION * TRANSPARENCY * IMMUTABILITY * SECURED BY CHRYPTOGRAPHY ### Blockchain series
In fact Blockchain Technology will be the Backbone or underlying infraestructure of the Internet 3.0 and IOT and AI will run on top of it.
